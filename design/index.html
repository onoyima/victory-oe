About Victory Oil & Energy

Victory Oil & Energy is a Pan African centric oil and energy organization with a diverse and growing portfolio of solutions and capabilities geared towards the delivery of efficient and reliable quality services within the Oil and Gas Market space in Angola, Nigeria and new market entry of Namibia.

 

VOE is well positioned with its affiliates and partners strategically positioned in the USA. UK and South Africa to better serve the needs of its growing client base, in offering solutions within Technical services, inspection services, Earth science services, Training solutions, Engineering solutions and Project management services.

VOE continues to shape itself as a market leader in its served locations, relative to the infusion of local content derivatives with international standards in its offerings. Our solutions unlock the ability to transform, transfer and transport energy efficiently and sustainably whilst capturing and reducing emissions, reducing environmental impact whilst maximizing efficiency, productivity, and reliability.

The heart of our company lies in our dedicated team of professionals who bring their diverse skills and expertise to every project we undertake. From our visionary founders to our skilled engineers, geologists, and support staff, each member of the VOE family plays a crucial role in shaping our success and upholding our commitment to excellence. VOEs core focus is rooted on its unwavering commitment to safety and quality execution of its services without losing touch on the need for sustainable local content development.
