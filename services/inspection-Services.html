Inspection Services

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

Our Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services offer a non-intrusive approach to inspecting materials, components, and structures. Utilizing advanced techniques such as ultrasonic testing, radiography, magnetic particle inspection, and visual inspection, our certified NDT inspectors detect flaws, defects, and irregularities without causing any damage. This ensures the integrity and reliability of your assets while minimizing downtime and costly repairs.

OCTG Inspection

Oil Country Tubular Goods (OCTG) play a critical role in the oil and gas industry, and our OCTG inspection services are designed to ensure their quality and compliance. Our inspectors meticulously assess the condition, dimensions, and performance characteristics of OCTG products, including casing, tubing, and drill pipes. With our expertise and adherence to industry standards, we help you make informed decisions and maintain the highest levels of safety and efficiency.

Distributed Goods Inspection

In the era of global supply chains, ensuring the quality and conformity of distributed goods is paramount. Our distributed goods inspection services provide comprehensive assessments of products, components, and materials at various stages of the supply chain. Our inspectors verify compliance with specifications, conduct quality control checks, and perform visual inspections to ensure that goods meet your requirements and adhere to regulatory standards.
