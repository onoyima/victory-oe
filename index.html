About Us

Victory Oil & Energy

Victory Oil & Energy(VOE) is a Pan-African oil and energy organization with a diverse portfolio of solutions designed to deliver efficient and reliable services within the Oil and Gas markets in Angola, Nigeria, and Namibia. VOE offers a range of services, including Technical services, Inspection services, Earth science services, Training solutions, Engineering solutions, and Project management services.

VOE is committed to combining local content with international standards to lead in its served locations. Our solutions focus on exploration, sourcing, and producing energy efficiently while minimizing environmental impact and maximizing productivity..

At the heart of VOE is a dedicated team of professionals who drive our success, upholding our commitment to safety, quality, and sustainable development in every project
