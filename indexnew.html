About Us

Victory Oil & Energy

Victory Oil & Energy(VOE) is a Pan-African oil and energy organization with a diverse portfolio of solutions designed to deliver efficient and reliable services within the Oil and Gas markets. VOE offers a range of services, including Technical services, Inspection services, Earth science services, Training solutions, Engineering solutions, and Project management services.

VOE is committed to combining local content with international standards to lead in its served locations. Our solutions focus on exploration, sourcing, and producing energy efficiently while minimizing environmental impact and maximizing productivity.

At the heart of VOE is a dedicated team of professionals who drive our success, upholding our commitment to safety, quality, and sustainable development in every project.

Vision: To be a leading provider of Oil and Gas & Energy Solutions, trusted by our partners and clients for our staunch commitment to excellence, compliance and innovation.

Mission: We are strongly committed to achieving our long-term strategy of becoming the leading provider of multi-service solutions that are innovative, streamlined, sustainable and operationally excellent.

HSE: To create a culture of safety where everyone is valued and respected, and accidents are prevented.

SERVICE QUALILTY: To create a customer-centric culture where everyone is committed to providing exceptional service.

OUR PEOPLE: To create a workplace where employees are valued, respected, and empowered to achieve their full potential.

COMMUNITY: To spread impact in the communities where we operate and leave them in a better state than we found them.

INTEGRITY: Our unwavering commitment to honesty, transparency, and ethical practices forms the foundation of everything we do, ensuring trust, respect, and long-lasting relationships.

QUALITY: Our relentless pursuit of excellence drives us to deliver solutions that surpass expectations, inspiring confidence and creating lasting value for our customers.

ONE TEAM, ONE MISSION: Together, we achieve greatness. Embracing collaboration, mutual support, and diverse perspectives, we unite our strengths to accomplish shared goals, fuel innovation, and foster an empowering work environment.

INNOVATION: We are committed to pushing boundaries & embracing creativity.

LEADERSHIP: We inspire, empower, and guide. With visionary thinking, integrity, and a commitment to excellence, we cultivate leaders at all levels, driving positive change, fostering growth, and making a meaningful impact in our industry and beyond.
