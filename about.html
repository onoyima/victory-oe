About Us

Pioneering Excellence in the Upstream Sector of the Oil & Gas and Energy market space

Victory Oil & Energy

We are, a Pan African centric oil and energy organization with a diverse and growing portfolio of solutions and capabilities geared towards the delivery of efficient and reliable quality services within the Oil and Gas Market space in Angola, Nigeria and new market entry of Namibia.

VOE is well positioned with its affiliates and partners strategically positioned in the USA. UK and South Africa to better serve the needs of its growing client base, in offering solutions within Technical services, inspection services, Earth science services, Training solution.

VOE continues to shape itself as a market leader in its served locations, relative to the infusion of local content derivatives with international standards in its offerings. Our solutions unlock the ability to transform, transfer and transport energy efficiently and sustainably whilst capturing and reducing emissions, reducing environmental impact whilst maximizing efficiency, productivity, and reliability.

The heart of our company lies in our dedicated team of professionals who bring their diverse skills and expertise to every project we undertake. From our visionary founders to our skilled engineers, geologists, and support staff, each member of the VOE family plays a crucial role in shaping our success and upholding our commitment to excellence. VOEs core focus is rooted on its unwavering commitment to safety and quality execution of its services without losing touch on the need for sustainable local content development.

Vision: To be a leading global provider of Oil and Gas & Energy Solutions, trusted by our partners and clients for our staunch commitment to excellence, compliance and innovation.

Mission: We are strongly committed to achieving our long-term strategy of becoming the leading provider of multi-service solutions that are innovative, streamlined, sustainable and operationally excellent.

HSE: To create a culture of safety where everyone is valued and respected, and accidents are prevented.

SERVICE QUALILTY: To create a customer-centric culture where everyone is committed to providing exceptional service.

OUR PEOPLE: To create a workplace where employees are valued, respected, and empowered to achieve their full potential.

COMMUNITY: To spread impact in the communities where we operate and leave them in a better state than we found them.

INTEGRITY: Our unwavering commitment to honesty, transparency, and ethical practices forms the foundation of everything we do, ensuring trust, respect, and long-lasting relationships.

QUALITY: Our relentless pursuit of excellence drives us to deliver solutions that surpass expectations, inspiring confidence and creating lasting value for our customers.

TEAMWORK: Together, we achieve greatness. Embracing collaboration, mutual support, and diverse perspectives, we unite our strengths to accomplish shared goals, fuel innovation, and foster an empowering work environment.

INNOVATION: We are committed to pushing boundaries & embracing creativity.

LEADERSHIP: We inspire, empower, and guide. With visionary thinking, integrity, and a commitment to excellence, we cultivate leaders at all levels, driving positive change, fostering growth, and making a meaningful impact in our industry and beyond.
